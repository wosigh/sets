A set consists of three cards which satisfy all of these conditions:
  • They all have the same number, or they have three different numbers.
  • They all have the same symbol, or they have three different symbols.
  • They all have the same shading, or they have three different shadings.
  • They all have the same color, or they have three different colors.
The rules of Set are summarized by: If you can sort a group of three cards into "Two of ____ and one of _____," then it is not a set. One example of a set would be these three cards:
  • One red striped diamond
  • Two red solid diamonds
  • Three red open diamonds
